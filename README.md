# Customer Experience Systems

This repository explores customer experience systems, workflow design, and operational structures that support better customer interactions and service consistency.

The focus is on designing systems that improve:
- communication clarity,
- response consistency,
- customer visibility,
- and operational alignment.

---

# Areas Covered

## Customer Journey Design
- Customer touchpoints
- Service interaction flows
- Communication processes

## Support Operations
- Escalation workflows
- Case management
- SLA visibility

## CRM & Customer Experience
- CRM workflow alignment
- Customer data visibility
- Follow-up systems

## Operational Challenges
- Delayed communication
- Workflow inefficiencies
- Process inconsistency
- Customer friction points
